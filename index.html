Visitors
In this field you can input how many visitors your website has or how many you want to have. You can chose between visitors per month or visitors per day. This is the basis to calculate the income from AdSense.
Pageviews per Visitor
This field is used to specify how many individual pages a visitor of you website sees.
CPC
CPC means Cost Per Click. This is the amount of money you earn when a visitor clicks on an ad.

You can find a detailed explanation and typical values for pageviews in the FAQ section.
CTR
CTR means Click Through Rate. It is the percentage of visitors which click on a AdSense ad.

You can find a detailed explanation and typical values for pageviews in the FAQ section.
CPM
CPM means Cost Per Million. It describes how much money you earn from 1000 page views. The CPM value is not editable since it islcalculated from the CPC and CTR.
CPM = CPC * CTR * 1000.

You can find a detailed explanation and typical values for pageviews in the FAQ section.
Conversion Rate
How many people buy the product you are advertising after they click on your affiliate link?
Offer Value
How much money do you get if a visitor buys the product that you are advertising?
